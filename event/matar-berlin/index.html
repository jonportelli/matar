Info

After a globe trotting year of parties, MATAR returns on New Year's Eve 2018 for a 24 hour marathon with another carefully curated line-up of established talents and exciting new artists.

At the center of this night are the ecstatic escalating grooves of the British trio Factory Floor.

On the occasion of their highly anticipated debut album on DFA Records, they finally bring their dynamic live sound to Berlin.

Somewhere between Throbbing Gristle classics, post-punk heroes like Joy Division, and excepter's trippy Endlospsychedelik, Factory Floor find their very own pop design.

Rightfully, they are considered to be the currently most exciting band from the UK. Between Dancefloor and Post-Industrial Factory Floor act as a hinge this evening, combining the shimmering synth pop of East India Youth and Hyetal with Reliq's hypnotic noisock and the narcotic drone beats of N ≠ E Resident Opium Hum. The night will be rounded off by a DJ set by Innen + Aussen, the joint project of nd_baumecker and Jenus Baumecker.

The venue is a colossal and inspiring East German structure dating back to 1951 that used to be the largest radio-broadcasting site in the world, and today is a treasure trove for all sorts of artists.

How do I get to the venue?

Address: Nalepastrasse 18, 12459 Berlin.

Are tickets available at the door?

There is no guarantee that tickets will be available at the door, but further information will be posted on the event page on Saturday.

Is there an ATM at the venue?

Yes, there will be an ATM. But it will save you time if you bring all the cash that you might need with you to the event.

Is it possible to pay with card at the bar?

No. Only cash is accepted at the bar.

Is there food available?

Yes there will be food stands with hot food available to purchase.

Is it possible to leave and re-enter the event?

No, once you leave you will not be allowed to re-enter, regardless of what type of ticket you have.

Is there a cloakroom?

Yes there is a cloakroom for you to store coats and bags. There will be a small fee. There are different cloakrooms for regular and premium ticket holders.

I have a premium ticket - which entrance do I use?

Follow signs to the PREMIUM entrance.

I have a regular pre-sale ticket - which entrance do I use?

Follow signs to PRE-SALE.

Is there wheelchair access to the venue?

All parts of the venue are accessible by wheelchair apart from the premium dance platforms/backstage area. The disabled toilet is located to the right hand side as you enter the venue from the regular pre-sale entrance.
