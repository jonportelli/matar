MATAR returns to London to join LDN E4 for their 10th anniversary for a one of a kind stage hosting.

MATAR will bring their carefully curated line ups and special atmosphere to create the unique MATAR experience.

LDN E4 turns the lush green Randall´s Island Park into every festival-goers dream.

Located along the East River between Manhattan, Queens and the Bronx, the island is centrally located in London.

AGE: 18+

After a massive day of MATAR x LDN E4 on Sunday, September 2nd, we carry on straight after at Analog, Brooklyn to finish off the weekend the MATAR way.
