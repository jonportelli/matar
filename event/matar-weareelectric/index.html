Sub:stance should always be a party that represents UK electronic music in Berlin: the self-perpetuating hardcore contiuum. Since the musical perspective of the young British and the sound that runs on Saturdays in the Berghain anyway, but have now largely approximated, there will be Sub: Stance only twice a year. Of course always with the Sub: Stance organizer Scuba, who has reinvented himself on his new album Personality with straight beats, reveling surfaces and occasional diva vocal and trance insertions. Dirty warehouse smashers can be heard from Boddika, the solo project of Al Bleek, who otherwise focuses on electro with Damon Kirkham as Instra: Mental. Also there: the Manchester Drum'n'Bass pioneer Marcus Intalex, Appleblim and a joint DJ set Silkie and Quest: heavy bass with a dreamy surface. The Panorama Bar will feature New York disco and house legend Francois Kevorkian, who has been pulling the strings for generations in the Big Apple for generations, from Paradise Garage, Studio 54, Body & Soul, to his current Monday club Deep Space NYC.
